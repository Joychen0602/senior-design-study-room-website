Introduction

The Study Room Environmental Analytics System is a smart system that helps students choose better study spaces. It combines a camera and environmental sensors to detect seat availability and measure room conditions such as temperature, humidity, and lighting. The system processes this data on an edge device and sends the results to the cloud, where it can be accessed through a user interface.

Problem & Motivation

Students often struggle to find suitable study spaces and there is no easy way to check conditions before arriving. This project is motivated by the need to provide both occupancy and environmental information in one system. Therefore, this system helps students save time and choose more comfortable places to study.
